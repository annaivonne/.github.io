About me

Born in 1998, I am a passionate reader and sports enthusiast. When not immersed in academic pursuits, I enjoy swimming, playing badminton, table tennis, and tennis to stay active and energized. I also enjoy listening to classical music and playing the violin, piano and Erhu. Reading novels is my ultimate escape, allowing me to explore diverse worlds and perspectives through literature.

Creative Foods

I love experimenting in the kitchen with fusion ideas, blending traditional flavors with modern twists. Here are some of my favorite creative recipes:

  • Avocado Egg Tart (牛油果蛋挞): A creamy, healthy spin on classic egg tarts, incorporating ripe avocado for a smooth, nutrient-packed filling.
  • Soy Milk Egg Tart (豆浆蛋挞): Plant-based twist using soy milk for a lighter, dairy-free version that's subtly sweet and aromatic.
  • Scrambled Egg Shrimp Pancake (滑蛋鲜虾饼): Fluffy scrambled eggs mixed with fresh shrimp in a crispy pancake, perfect for a quick savory bite.
