I am a recent PhD graduate in Accounting from Fudan University, serving as a Visiting Scholar at the University of Texas at Dallas in 2024 . I earned my B.A. in Accounting from the U.S. Accounting Program (Elite Class) at Shanghai University of Finance and Economics in 2019, with a thesis titled “Does Stock Connect Bring Information or Noise?”

My research interests lie in information disclosure, management accounting, innovation and capital markets, and the Sci-Tech Innovation Board registration system. I have published in leading Chinese journals and presented at international conferences, including the 2024 AAA Annual Meeting in Washington D.C. as a discussant and the 2025 Accounting and Business Research Meeting. My work on the Sci-Tech Innovation Board's IPO arrangements and firm innovation has been recognized for its contributions to understanding listing standards' effectiveness.

I have served as a teaching assistant for courses such as Tax Law (MBA), Financial Statement Analysis (MBA), Accounting (Undergraduate), and Advanced Accounting. My fieldwork includes data collection on asset securitization and real estate loan ratings, participation in the Shanghai University of Finance and Economics' "Thousand Villages Survey" in Gansu Province, and a National Bureau of Statistics survey on the business environment in Shanghai's startups.

I possess strong technical skills in STATA, SAS, and Python (including Deep Learning), along with AI tools like ChatGPT and DeepSeek. My language proficiency includes CET-6 (652), TOEFL 100, GMAT 680, and IELTS 7.0, and I have won awards such as the Third Prize in Fudan University's English Speech Competition. During my PhD, I received the Fudan University Outstanding Student Scholarship (2019–2023), Shanghai Excellent Graduate (2019), and Third Prize in the Fudan PhD Forum for Outstanding Papers.

In 2025, I co-authored papers presented at the Accounting and Business Research Conference at Fudan University, furthering my research on dividends and firm value in the context of China's Science and Technology Innovation Board.
