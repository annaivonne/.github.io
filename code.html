Research and Technical Expertise

I received my PhD in Accounting from Fudan University in 2026 and was a Visiting Scholar at the University of Texas at Dallas. My research focuses on intelligent accounting, management accounting, financial reporting, and financial statement analysis. My research interest in PhD stage is Chinese unique institutions, such as the registration reform and regulations of Science and Technology Board. I also explore applications of data analytics, machine learning, and AI (including ChatGPT) in accounting and corporate finance, such as dividend policies.

My recent work examines the interactions between intelligent accounting, financial statement analysis, and management accounting to improve corporate decision-making, governance, and capital market efficiency. I have participated and discussed at major conferences including the AAA Annual Meeting.

At Fudan University and UTD, I was a teaching assistent in advanced financial accounting, management accounting, financial statement analysis, tax and intelligent accounting. I bring strong technical expertise to my research, including Python (deep learning), STATA, SAS, ChatGPT, DeepSeek, and advanced data analysis.
